projects

Quantm By Solexa

Quantm aims to simplify existing approaches to telemedicine by providing an all-in-one utility for both healthcare providers and patients to monitor,track, and consult regarding the progression of an array of conditions.

Horizon

Horizon is an all-in-one web application for your long term health needs. It helps you store information about allergies, keep track of prescriptions, and log side-effects. Created at a hackathon!

NailSight

A Convolutional Neural Network that detects and diagnosises diseases from a simple picture of your fingernail! This application is currently under construction.
